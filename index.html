Frequently Answered Questions

Do we have to install software on our local computers?

No. Dentflow is entirely web-based and runs in any modern browser on standard PCs, Macs, or tablets. You do not need any local servers or specialized IT support contracts.

How does Dentflow sync with our patient records?

You can securely upload your active patient list as a standard CSV file directly in the onboarding panel. There are no complicated or fragile database bridges to break.

How are automated texts kept compliant?

Every text recall and scheduling trigger complies with strict TCPA regulations, with automated opt-out features baked in.
